## 6. Content Infrastructure Recommendations

1. **Add a blog/content section** at `/blog` or `/guides`. Currently impossible to rank for long-tail keywords without this.
2. **Fix the root redirect** — serving content at `/landing` instead of `/` means every inbound link loses equity to a redirect. Consider moving landing page to `/` with server-side auth detection via middleware.
3. **Target India explicitly** — the app UI already shows INR. Add India-specific metadata, create the India landing page, and consider `/in/` localised pages for the biggest keywords.
4. **Build internal links** — once you have 4–5 pages, link between them. The Splitwise Alternative page links to the roommates page, which links to the how-to guide, etc.
5. **Claim relevant directories** — G2, AlternativeTo, ProductHunt, SoftwareSuggest. These sites appear in SERPs and can send referral traffic while building domain authority.
